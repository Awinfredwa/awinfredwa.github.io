AI-Assisted Profile Customization

JanitorAI users were already customizing their profiles with CSS, but the workflow was mostly manual and easiest for power users. I helped turn that into an assisted product experience with an editor, live preview, edit history, and AI editing flow that could help users generate or revise profile styles from natural-language requests.

The important product constraint was safety: users needed room to experiment creatively without breaking their profile or losing previous work. The final workflow emphasized preview before publish, rollback through edit history, guardrails around AI-assisted output, and controlled rollout through experiments.

What I Built

I built the React and TypeScript product surface around the profile editor workflow: CSS editing, previewing changes, browsing prior edits, and using AI assistance without taking control away from users who wanted to write CSS directly.

Company-Safe Summary

Because this was company work, I am keeping implementation details high-level. The core takeaway is the product engineering shape: take a flexible but fragile user workflow, wrap it in clear controls, and make it reliable enough for daily use on a large consumer platform.
